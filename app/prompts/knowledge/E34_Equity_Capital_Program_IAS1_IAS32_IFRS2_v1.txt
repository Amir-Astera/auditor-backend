[1. EQUITY STRUCTURE]

{{equity_structure}}

Typical components:
- Share capital / statutory capital  
- Share premium  
- Retained earnings  
- Other reserves:  
  * revaluation reserves  
  * FVOCI reserve  
  * FX translation reserve  
  * hedging reserves  
- Treasury shares  
- Non-controlling interests

[2. RISK ASSESSMENT — IAS 32 & IFRS 2]

{{risk_assessment}}

Key risks:
- Incorrect equity vs liability classification  
- Missing disclosure of share classes  
- Incorrect treasury share accounting  
- Incorrect share-based payments valuation  
- Missing approvals for dividends  
- Incorrect OCI movements  
- Incorrect classification of instruments with put options  

Fraud risks:
- Manipulating distributable reserves  
- Incorrectly presenting liabilities as equity  
- Using complex shareholder structures for concealment
