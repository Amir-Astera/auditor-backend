[1. INTANGIBLE ASSET CATEGORIES]

{{intangible_categories}}

Typical categories:
- Software (internally developed or acquired)  
- Licenses  
- Trademarks & brands  
- Customer relationships  
- Patents & technology  
- Capitalized development costs  
- Contractual rights  
- Goodwill from business combinations

[2. RISK ASSESSMENT — IAS 38, IAS 36, IFRS 3]

{{risk_assessment}}

Key risks:
- Incorrect capitalization of costs (common issue)  
- Impairment indicators ignored  
- Useful lives unrealistic  
- Missing amortization  
- Unsupported DCF models for impairment  
- Incorrect PPA valuation  
- Goodwill not allocated to CGUs properly  
- Internally generated intangible assets incorrectly recognized  

Fraud risks:
- Overstating intangibles to manipulate EBITDA  
- Manipulating impairment assumptions  
- Capitalizing expenses to reduce current year costs

[4. CAPITALIZATION TESTING (IAS 38)**]

{{capitalization_testing}}

Rules:
- Research → expense  
- Development → capitalize only if criteria met  

Tests:
- Inspect project phase  
- Validate technical and commercial feasibility  
- Evaluate intention to complete  
- Test ability to use or sell asset  
- Verify reliable measurement of costs  
- Reconcile development costs to GL / payroll / invoices  

Schema:

CAPITALIZATION_SCHEMA:
  id: "<CAPxx>"
  intangible_id: "<INTxx>"
  phase: "<research|development>"
  criteria_met: "<true|false>"
  capitalized_cost: "<numeric>"
  expensed_cost: "<numeric>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"

[7. IMPAIRMENT TESTING FOR CGUs (IAS 36)**]

{{impairment_cgu}}

Tests:
- Identify external/internal impairment indicators  
- Obtain management’s impairment model  
- Evaluate model logic (DCF)  
- Test cash flow projections  
- Test discount rates (WACC testing → link to E24 inputs)  
- Test terminal value  
- Evaluate sensitivity analysis  
- Compare carrying amount vs recoverable amount  

Recovery models:
- Value in use (VIU)  
- Fair value less costs of disposal (FVLCD)  

Schema:

CGU_IMPAIRMENT_SCHEMA:
  id: "<CGUxx>"
  cgu_name: "<string>"
  carrying_amount: "<numeric>"
  recoverable_amount: "<numeric>"
  impairment_needed: "<true|false>"
  impairment_amount: "<numeric>"
  evidence_ref: "<WP ref>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"

[9. PURCHASE PRICE ALLOCATION (PPA) REVIEW — IFRS 3]

{{ppa_review}}

Tests:
- Evaluate identification of intangible assets  
- Evaluate fair value assumptions used

  Consideration – FV net assets acquired = Goodwill  
- Test earn-out liabilities (link E27 provisions if needed)  
- Test contingent consideration valuation
