{{instrument_classes}}

Include:
- cash  
- trade receivables

[2. RISK ASSESSMENT — IFRS 7]

{{risk_assessment}}

Key risks:
- Wrong classification of instruments  
- Missing credit risk disclosures  
- Missing liquidity risk maturity analysis  
- Wrong ECL levels (Stage 1/2/3)  
- Missing fair value hierarchy  
- Missing sensitivity analysis  
- Hedge disclosures incomplete  
- Derecognition (factoring/securitization) incorrectly treated  

Fraud risks:
- Understating ECL  
- Omitting derivative exposures  
- Manipulating fair value level classification  
- Hiding off-balance-sheet risks

[5. EXPECTED CREDIT LOSS (ECL) DISCLOSURES — IFRS 9 REQUIRED]

{{ecl_disclosures}}

- Forward-looking information  
- Sensitivity analysis  
- Write-offs
