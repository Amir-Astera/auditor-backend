DOCUMENT: KEY AUDIT MATTER (KAM) SKELETONS (ISA 701)

Purpose
-------
This document provides ready-to-use skeletons for drafting **Key Audit Matters (KAMs)** in accordance with ISA 701.  
It ensures consistency, completeness, and linkage with underlying audit evidence and judgments.  
The structure is adaptable across industries and clients, with placeholders for entity-specific details.

Relevant Standards:
- ISA 701: Communicating Key Audit Matters in the Independent Auditor’s Report
- ISA 260 / 265: Communication with Those Charged with Governance
- ISA 570: Going Concern
- ISA 540: Accounting Estimates
- ISA 240: Fraud Considerations
- Cross-references: ISA 330 (responses to risks), ISA 705/706 (modifications, emphasis)

─────────────────────────────────────────────
General Principles for KAMs
---------------------------
- KAMs are selected from matters communicated to TCWG that required significant auditor attention.
- Not all significant risks are KAMs, but all KAMs arise from matters of most significance.
- Language must be **entity-specific, balanced, informative, not boilerplate**.
- KAMs describe:  
  1. Why the matter was considered a KAM.  
  2. How it was addressed in the audit.  
  3. Reference to related disclosures in the FS.  
- Avoid: disclosing original information not publicly disclosed by the entity.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
KAM Skeleton Structure
----------------------

Heading: [Descriptive Title of the Matter]

Why This Matter Was Considered a KAM
- Summarize the underlying risk or judgment.
- Refer to financial statement areas impacted.
- Highlight complexity, subjectivity, or potential material impact.

How We Addressed the Matter in the Audit
- Outline audit procedures performed (substantive + control testing).
- Emphasize areas of auditor judgment and professional skepticism.
- Link to evidence obtained.

Reference
- Direct user to related note(s) in the FS.

Русское пояснение (для внутреннего использования)
-------------------------------------------------
KAM (ключевые вопросы аудита) — это те вопросы, которые:  
- были наиболее значимыми при аудите отчётности,  
- потребовали повышенного внимания аудитора,  
- должны быть раскрыты в аудиторском заключении для прозрачности.  

Каждый KAM описывается по схеме:  
1. Почему это KAM (сложность, суждение, риск).  
2. Как аудитор ответил на этот вопрос (процедуры, доказательства).  
3. Ссылка на примечания отчётности.  

Документ содержит шаблоны (skeletons) и быстрые фразы (Quick Insert), которые можно использовать при подготовке текста.
