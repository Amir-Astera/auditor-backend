CONSOLIDATION & GROUP AUDIT — IFRS 10 • IFRS 12 • IAS 28  
Client: {{client_name}}  
Period Ended: {{period_end}}

This audit program covers the determination of control, consolidation procedures,
group adjustments, eliminations, structured entities, associates, and required disclosures.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[1. GROUP STRUCTURE OVERVIEW]

{{group_structure}}

Include:
- list of subsidiaries  
- list of associates  
- list of joint ventures  
- SPVs and structured entities  
- ownership percentages  
- voting rights vs economic rights  
- agreements granting control  

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[2. COMPONENT LIST & ISA 600 REQUIREMENTS]

{{component_list}}

Tests:
- Identify significant components (financial or business significance)  
- Identify components with significant risks  
- Define component materiality  
- Communicate instructions to component auditors  

Schema:

COMPONENT_SCHEMA:
  id: "<COMPxx>"
  entity_name: "<string>"
  ownership: "<numeric>"
  auditor: "<internal|external>"
  component_materiality: "<numeric>"
  significance: "<financial|risk>"
  evidence_ref: "<WP ref>"

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[3. RISK ASSESSMENT — IFRS 10 + ISA 600]

{{risk_assessment}}

Key risks:
- Incorrect assessment of control  
- Missing consolidation of controlled SPVs  
- Incorrect classification as associate/JV  
- Incorrect eliminations  
- Manipulation of intercompany profit  
- Incorrect NCI measurement  
- Inconsistent accounting policies  
- Missing IFRS 12 disclosures  

Fraud risks:
- Hiding liabilities in unconsolidated entities  
- Manipulating control structure  
- Window dressing through intercompany sales  

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[4. CONTROL ASSESSMENT (IFRS 10)**]

{{control_assessment}}

Control requires:
1. **Power** over investee  
2. **Exposure to variable returns**  
3. **Ability to affect returns**  

Tests:
- Voting rights vs de facto control  
- Potential rights (options, convertibles)  
- Contractual arrangements  
- Removal rights / protective rights  
- Purpose and design of structured entities  

Schema:

CONTROL_SCHEMA:
  id: "<CTRLxx>"
  entity_name: "<string>"
  power: "<true|false>"
  exposure_to_returns: "<true|false>"
  ability_to_affect_returns: "<true|false>"
  control_exists: "<true|false>"
  evidence_ref: "<WP ref>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[5. CONSOLIDATION PROCEDURES**

{{consolidation_procedures}}

Procedures:
- Align reporting dates (unless IFRS 10 exception applies)  
- Align accounting policies  
- Full consolidation for subsidiaries  
- Proportionate accounting for JVs (if applicable)  
- Equity method for associates (IAS 28)  

Schema:

CONSOLIDATION_SCHEMA:
  id: "<CONSxx>"
  entity_name: "<string>"
  method: "<full|equity|none>"
  policy_alignment_ok: "<true|false>"
  reporting_date_alignment: "<true|false>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[6. NON-CONTROLLING INTEREST (NCI) TESTING]

{{nci_testing}}

Tests:
- FV vs proportionate share approach (IFRS 3)  
- Recalculate NCI movements  
- Test profit attribution  
- Test OCI attribution  

Schema:

NCI_SCHEMA:
  id: "<NCIxx>"
  entity_name: "<string>"
  method: "<fair_value|proportionate>"
  opening_balance: "<numeric>"
  profit_attributable: "<numeric>"
  OCI_attributable: "<numeric>"
  closing_balance: "<numeric>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
[7. INTERCOMPANY ELIMINATIONS (CRITICAL)**]

{{intercompany_eliminations}}

Required eliminations:
- Intercompany sales  
- Intercompany receivables/payables (E21 & E22 linkage)  
- Intercompany loans  
- Intercompany interest  
- Unrealized profits in inventory (IAS 2 link)  
- Unrealized profits in PPE (IAS 16 link)  
- Dividends from subsidiaries  

Schema:

ELIMINATION_SCHEMA:
  id: "<ELIMxx>"
  transaction_type: "<sale|loan|interest|inventory|PPE|dividend>"
  entity_from: "<string>"
  entity_to: "<string>"
  amount: "<numeric>"
  unrealized_profit: "<numeric>"
  eliminated: "<true|false>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"
