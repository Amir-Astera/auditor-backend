DOCUMENT: COMMUNICATIONS WITH THOSE CHARGED WITH GOVERNANCE (TCWG) — ISA 260/265/701/570 PACK (B4-LEVEL)

Purpose
-------
This pack standardizes all required and value-adding communications with TCWG across the audit lifecycle. It defines:
- WHAT must be communicated (required by ISA + best practice),
- WHEN and HOW to communicate (calendar, formats),
- WHO owns each item (client/audit),
- QUALITY GATES and traceability to working papers and FS disclosures.

Applicable Standards
- ISA 260 (Revised): Communication with TCWG
- ISA 265: Communicating Deficiencies in Internal Control
- ISA 701: Communicating Key Audit Matters
- ISA 570: Going Concern
- ISA 240/250: Fraud; Laws & Regulations (NOCLAR)
- ISA 540/330/315/520/530/560/580: Estimates; Responses; Risk; Analytics; Sampling; Subsequent Events; Written Representations
Cross-refs: 02_StyleGuide, 03_ISA_RoutingPrompts, 04_Risk_Library_by_Cycle, 05_Materiality_Playbook, 06_Sampling_Methods_ISA530, 07_Legal_Matrix, 08_PBC_Master_List, 09_Audit_Templates, 10_Management_Rep_Full_ISA580, 12_KAM_Skeletons, 13_Forensic_RedFlags, 14_Glossary, 15_Model_IO_Guide

PACK COMPONENTS (DELIVERABLE SET)
---------------------------------
1) TCWG Planning Communication (Deck/Memo) — ISA 260
   - Scope & timing, group/locations, component materiality
   - Overall materiality, performance materiality, CT (clearly trivial threshold)
   - Significant risks & fraud risks; planned responses; use of specialists
   - Independence threats & safeguards; team rotation
   - Reporting approach (KAM candidates, modified opinion triggers)
   - PBC calendar, dependencies, logistics

2) Interim Update (1–2 pages or short deck)
   - Status vs plan; preliminary findings; changes to risk assessment/materiality
   - Emerging matters (legal, regulatory, going concern indicators)
   - Issues requiring TCWG decisions

3) Year-End Results Communication (Deck + Letter) — ISA 260
   - Significant findings; qualitative aspects of accounting practices
   - Corrected & uncorrected misstatements (SUM)
   - Judgments & estimates (ISA 540), bias indicators
   - Related parties; NOCLAR; fraud matters; deficiencies overview
   - Going concern evaluation; subsequent events
   - Draft auditor’s report (incl. KAMs) and reporting logistics

4) Internal Control Deficiencies Letter — ISA 265
   - Significant deficiencies (definition, per-process detail, effects, root cause)
   - Other deficiencies (opportunities to strengthen)
   - Management responses & action plans

5) Independence Confirmation — Annual Written Statement
   - Threats (self-review, self-interest, familiarity, advocacy, intimidation)
   - Safeguards applied; conclusion of independence

6) Summary of Unadjusted Misstatements (SUM) — Appendix to 260 letter
   - Quantified and qualitative assessment; rollover/iron-curtain analysis
   - Recommendation to adjust; effect on opinion if not adjusted

7) KAM Pre-Read — ISA 701
   - Proposed KAM titles; “why KAM”; “how addressed”; FS note references
   - Link to 12_KAM_Skeletons and underlying evidence

8) Going Concern Communication — ISA 570
   - Summary of procedures & assumptions; sensitivity results; covenant headroom
   - Material uncertainty vs Emphasis of Matter decision tree

9) Subsequent Events & Legal Bring-Down — ISA 560/501
   - Through-date, bring-down of legal counsel and management reps
   - Confirmations status

10) TCWG Action Tracker & Decisions Log
   - Open items, decisions taken, deadlines, owners

─────────────────────────────────────────────
COMMUNICATIONS CALENDAR (PHASE-BASED)
-------------------------------------
Planning (PLN) — by [D-30 to D-10]
- Deliver 1) Planning Deck; 5) Independence Statement; proposed materiality & risks
Interim (INT) — as applicable
- Deliver 2) Interim Update; emerging KAMs
Year-End (YE) — fieldwork end ±1–3 days
- Deliver 3) Year-End Results Deck + 6) SUM draft; KAM pre-read
Close (CL) — pre-report issue
- Deliver 4) ISA 265 Letter; 8) Going Concern memo; 9) Bring-down status
Report (RP) — before signing
- Final 3) 260 Letter; final KAMs; logistics (signing, distribution)

Channels & Attendance
- Formal meetings (in person/VC); Chair of AC, CFO, CEO (as invited)
- Pre-reads sent ≥48 hours in advance; minutes prepared within 48 hours post-meeting

QUALITY GATES (B4-LEVEL)
------------------------
- Versioning: YYYYMMDD_CLIENT_TCWG_[DocType]_vNN (PDF for finals; PPTX/DOCX working)
- Cross-referencing: Each conclusion ties to WP refs, evidence IDs, and FS notes
- Materiality: Show OM, PM, CT; explain rationale and changes
- Independence: Explicit threats assessment + safeguards
- Actionability: For each finding — effect, root cause, required action, owner, due date
- Plain-English, entity-specific; avoid boilerplate; disclose no original info not in FS

TEMPLATES — OUTLINES & BOILERPLATE
----------------------------------

A) Planning Communication (ISA 260) — Deck Outline
1. Engagement Overview & Team (independence, rotation)
2. Scope & Timetable (group, components, reliance strategy)
3. Materiality: OM/PM/CT, benchmarks, rationale
4. Significant Risks & Fraud Risks; responses (link: 04_Risk_Library)
5. Use of Specialists (valuation, actuarial, IT)
6. Data & PBC Plan (link: 08_PBC_Master_List)
7. Reporting Approach (KAM candidates; modifications triggers)
8. Legal & Subsequent Events plan (link: 07_Legal_Matrix)
9. Communications cadence; needed decisions

B) Year-End Results Letter (ISA 260) — Sections
- Executive Summary (overall conclusion; status vs plan)
- Significant Findings (by area; effect on FS)
- Qualitative Aspects of Accounting Practices (policies, estimates, bias)
- Corrected & Uncorrected Misstatements (attach SUM)
- Fraud & NOCLAR Considerations (ISA 240/250)
- Related Parties & Unusual Transactions (ISA 550)
- Going Concern & Subsequent Events (ISA 570/560)
- Independence Confirmation (updated)
- KAMs to be communicated in the report (ISA 701)
- Other Reporting (Emphasis/Other Matter; modifications if any)
- Appendices: SUM; management letter points index; meeting minutes

C) ISA 265 — Control Deficiencies Letter (Structure)
- Purpose & Scope (definition of significant deficiency)
- Summary of Significant Deficiencies (table: process, condition, criterion, cause, consequence, recommendation)
- Other Deficiencies (improvement opportunities)
- Management Responses & Target Dates
- Auditor’s Responsibilities Limitation paragraph (ISA wording)
Note: Map to 03_ISA_RoutingPrompts for drafting tone; do not use “material weakness” terminology unless jurisdiction requires; use “significant deficiency” per ISA.

D) Summary of Unadjusted Misstatements (SUM) — Table
| # | FS Caption | Description | ΔPBT | ΔEquity | ΔEPS | CT? | Qualitative factors | Management decision | Auditor view | WP Ref |
De-minimis (CT) threshold: [insert per 05_Materiality_Playbook]. Provide aggregate effect and rollover/iron-curtain analysis.

E) Independence Confirmation — Paragraph (Short Form)
“We confirm that we are independent of [Entity] in accordance with relevant ethical requirements. We have assessed threats (self-interest, self-review, familiarity, advocacy, intimidation) and applied safeguards as described in Appendix A. We are not aware of any relationships or services that, in our professional judgment, bear on our independence.”

F) Going Concern Communication — Outline (ISA 570)
- Management’s assessment period and key assumptions
- Procedures performed (forecast testing, sensitivities, covenant headroom)
- Indicators observed; mitigating plans; post-balance developments
- Conclusion: No MU; MU with adequate disclosure; Inadequate disclosure (modification)
- Reporting implications (EoM vs MU paragraph; basis for modification)

G) KAM Pre-Read — One-Pager per Candidate (link 12_KAM_Skeletons)
- Title; Why KAM; How addressed; FS Note refs; Residual matters for TCWG decision

─────────────────────────────────────────────
TRIGGERS & ESCALATION LOGIC
---------------------------
- Fraud allegations / suspected NOCLAR → immediate TCWG notification route (ISA 240/250) per legal advice
- Scope limitation (e.g., absence of legal letters) → early alert to AC; consider modification (ISA 705)
- Significant deficiency detected → written communication on a timely basis, not just at year-end (ISA 265)
- Emerging going concern indicators → expedited meeting; agree disclosures and updates cadence
- Large unadjusted misstatement near OM → escalate with quantified effects and qualitative reasons

─────────────────────────────────────────────
DECISION TREES (CONDENSED)
--------------------------
A) KAM or Not? (ISA 701)
Matter of most significance communicated to TCWG? → Yes → Public interest/value to users? → Yes → Draft KAM using skeleton → Validate with partner/legal.

B) Deficiency Classification (ISA 265)
Control deficiency → Does it merit attention by TCWG due to likelihood/magnitude? → Yes → Significant deficiency; otherwise record as Other deficiency (still communicate if helpful).

C) Going Concern Reporting (ISA 570)
Material uncertainty? → Adequate disclosure → MU paragraph; Inadequate → Modified opinion; No MU but significant judgment → Consider EoM.

QUICK INSERT PHRASES (Tailor to Client)
---------------------------------------
- “We reassessed materiality to reflect updated performance and risk; PM is set at X% of OM to address aggregation risk.”
- “This matter required significant auditor attention due to complexity/estimation uncertainty and potential material impact.”
- “We recommend adjusting the uncorrected misstatements totaling [amount]; if not adjusted, the aggregate effect on [metric] would be [amount].”
- “We identified a significant deficiency in [process]; absent remediation, the risk of [misstatement/effect] remains elevated.”
- “Our independence assessment identified [threat]; safeguards applied include [actions]. We remain independent.”

RUSSIAN SUMMARY (для внутреннего пользования)
---------------------------------------------
Пакет TCWG — это стандартизированный набор коммуникаций с Лицами, отвечающими за корпоративное управление:
- Планирование (материальность, риски, независимость, план работ),
- Промежуточные обновления,
- Итоговые результаты (значимые выводы, корректировки/некорректировки, оценки, связанные стороны, NOCLAR, непрерывность, KAM),
- Письмо о недостатках внутреннего контроля (ISA 265),
- Подтверждение независимости,
- Предварительные KAM и логика отчётности (ISA 701),
- Лог последующих событий и юридическое «bring-down».

Каждый документ — с чёткими критериями качества и связями с рабочими документами и примечаниями к отчетности.
