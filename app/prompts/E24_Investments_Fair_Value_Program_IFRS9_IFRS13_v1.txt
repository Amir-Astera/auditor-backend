{{instrument_population}}

- Debt instruments (bonds, notes)  
- Equity instruments

- Term deposits  
- Loans issued  
- FVPL portfolios

[2. RISK ASSESSMENT — IFRS 9 + IFRS 13 + ISA 540]

{{risk_assessment}}

Key risks:
- Incorrect classification (AC / FVPL / FVOCI)  
- Incorrect business model assessment  
- Incorrect cash flow characteristics (SPPI test)  
- FV measurement errors (Level 2 & Level 3 inputs)  
- Unsupported valuation models  
- Manipulation of unobservable inputs (ISA 240 override risk)  
- Incorrect ECL calculation for debt instruments  
- Incorrect hedge accounting designation  

High-risk areas:
- Level 3 instruments  
- Complex derivatives  
- Structured products  
- Instruments without market quotes

[4. CLASSIFICATION TESTING — IFRS 9]

{{classification_testing}}

Auditor must test:
1. **Business Model Assessment**  
   - review management documentation  
   - inspect sales patterns  
   - inspect risk management strategy  

2. **SPPI Test (Solely Payments of Principal and Interest)**  
   Fail → classification must be FVPL.

3. **Classification consequences:**  
   | Type | Measurement |
   |------|-------------|
   | SPPI + Hold | Amortized Cost |
   | SPPI + Hold/Sell | FVOCI Debt |
   | Fail SPPI | FVPL |
   | Equity instrument | FVPL or irrevocable FVOCI |

Schema:

CLASSIFICATION_SCHEMA:
  id: "<CLxx>"
  instrument_id: "<FINxx>"
  business_model: "<text>"
  sppi: "<pass|fail>"
  classification_ok: "<true|false>"
  exception_flag: "<true|false>"

[5. FAIR VALUE HIERARCHY — IFRS 13]

{{fv_hierarchy}}

Definitions:
- **Level 1**: quoted prices in active markets  
- **Level 2**: observable inputs (yield curves, credit spreads)  
- **Level 3**: unobservable inputs (discount rates, growth, volatilities, liquidity adjustments)
