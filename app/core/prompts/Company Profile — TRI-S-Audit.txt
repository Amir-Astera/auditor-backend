Services & Offerings

EN:

Audit & Assurance: statutory/voluntary audits (IFRS, NAS, US GAAP under ISA/PCAOB), ESG/KPI assurance, AUP (ISRS 4400), forensic & environmental audits.

Investment Consulting: due diligence (financial/tax/operational), valuations (IVS, DCF/WACC), capital structuring, post-deal integration.

Business Intelligence: market research, PEP/sanctions/UBO checks, data scraping, dashboards, industry papers.

Advisory & Risk: governance (OECD), COSO ERM, tax optimisation, CFC/DTT.

Digital Finance & IT: ERP rollout, process automation, IFRS bridge tools, analytics.

RU:

Аудит и Assurance: обязательный/добровольный аудит (МСФО, NAS, US GAAP по ISA/PCAOB), ESG/KPI, согласованные процедуры (ISRS 4400), форензик и экологический аудит.
