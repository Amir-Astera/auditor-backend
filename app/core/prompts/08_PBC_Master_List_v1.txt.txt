DOCUMENT: PBC (Prepared-By-Client) MASTER LIST — ISA-GUIDED, B4-LEVEL

Purpose
-------
This document is the master catalogue of client deliverables required for an ISA-compliant audit. It defines:
- WHAT to obtain (by audit cycle and industry addenda),
- HOW it must be delivered (format, fields, quality gates),
- WHEN it is due (phase-based calendar),
- WHO is responsible (client owner / audit owner),
- TRACEABILITY to working papers, legal matrix, materiality, and reporting.

Standards & Cross-References
- ISA 210, 230, 240, 250, 260/265, 300, 315/540, 330, 501, 505, 520, 530, 560, 570, 580
- IFRS focus: IAS 1, 2, 7, 10, 12, 16, 19, 21, 23, 24, 36, 37, 38, 40; IFRS 15, 16, 17 (for insurers)

GLOBAL ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA (QUALITY GATES)
------------------------------------------
A. File Hygiene
- Formats: XLSX/CSV (UTF-8, comma or semicolon), PDF (searchable), DOCX; NO images for primary data.
- Naming: YYYYMMDD_PBCID_ShortName_vNN.ext  (e.g., 20250901_PBC-GL01_GL_Detail_v02.csv)
- Versioning: supply full replacement files; avoid partial deltas unless agreed.

B. Data Integrity
- TB ties to FS; provide comparative periods; no rounding in source data.
- Each data table includes: unique row ID, Document Date, Posting Date, Doc No, Account Code+Name, Amount DC, Currency, Source Module, UserID, Counterparty ID/Name.
- Master data dictionaries for codes (CoA, product, customer, vendor, location).

C. Completeness & Cut-off
- Close calendars (hard close/soft close dates); through-date for subsequent events (ISA 560).
- Reconciliations provided and signed by process owner.

D. Evidence & Authorization
- PDFs are scanned at 300+ dpi, text-searchable; approval signatures legible.
- Provide policy/procedure documents referenced in data (credit policy, revenue recognition memos, inventory policy, capitalization policy, etc.).

Status Codes (lifecycle)
- NR (Not Requested), RQ (Requested), RC (Received), RV (In Review), AC (Accepted), RJ (Rejected), SP (Superseded)

Priority
- C (Critical), H (High), M (Medium), L (Low)

CORE REQUESTS BY CYCLE (BASELINE)
---------------------------------

ENTITY & GOVERNANCE
- PBC-GOV01 Board/Audit Committee Minutes — FY+post-balance to report date; agendas, decisions, approvals (capital, div, litigation). Format: PDF searchable. Tests: completeness vs calendar.
- PBC-GOV02 Organizational Chart & Legal Structure — entities, ownership %, change log. XLSX + org PDF.
- PBC-GOV03 Policies — Accounting manual, revenue, credit, inventory, FA, impairment, FX, leases, CapEx, procurement, whistleblowing (NOCLAR). PDF.

LEGAL & SUBSEQUENT EVENTS (tie to 07_Legal_Matrix)
- PBC-LEG01 Legal Matters Register — asserted/unasserted claims, counterparties, probability, ranges. XLSX (fields per Legal Matrix).
- PBC-LEG02 External Counsel Letters — law firms list & confirmations; bring-down before report date.
- PBC-LEG03 Regulatory Correspondence — tax/regulatory notices, fines, inspections. PDF.
- PBC-LEG04 Subsequent Events Log — from balance date to report date, including approvals, M&A, impairments, financing. XLSX.

FINANCIAL STATEMENTS & CLOSING
- PBC-FS01 Trial Balance — year-end & comparatives, pre/post-adjustments; mapping to FS lines. XLSX.
- PBC-FS02 Chart of Accounts — code, name, type, parent, IFRS map. XLSX.
- PBC-FS03 Posting Period Calendar — open/close status by module. XLSX.
- PBC-FS04 Journal Entry Listing — full FY; fields: JE ID, date, user, source, amounts DC, description, reversal flag. CSV/XLSX.
- PBC-FS05 Manual JEs (Top Adjustments) — management entries > materiality; rationale & approvals. XLSX+PDF.
- PBC-FS06 FS Draft & Notes — latest management draft with note numbers. DOCX/PDF.

CASH & BANK (ISA 505)
- PBC-CB01 Bank Account Master & Signatories — list w/ currencies, banks, IBAN/SWIFT. XLSX.
- PBC-CB02 Bank Statements — FY + Jan/Feb after YE for roll-forward. PDF/e-statements.
- PBC-CB03 Bank Reconciliations — YE reconciliations, outstanding items aging. XLSX.
- PBC-CB04 External Bank Confirmations — contact sheet for confirms; letters to be sent by auditor. XLSX/PDF.

REVENUE & ACCOUNTS RECEIVABLE (IFRS 15)
- PBC-REV01 Revenue by Month/Product/Customer — net/gross, discounts, returns, VAT. XLSX.
- PBC-REV02 Contract/Order Repository — top N contracts (by value/risk), standard T&Cs, rebate programs. PDF.
- PBC-REV03 AR Subledger & Aging — customer ID, terms, credit limit, balance aging buckets. XLSX.
- PBC-REV04 Price Lists, Rebates, Bonuses — schemes, accrual calcs. XLSX/PDF.
- PBC-REV05 Shipping/Delivery Docs (Sample Pulls) — invoices, delivery notes, CMR/waybills per audit samples. PDF.

PURCHASING & ACCOUNTS PAYABLE
- PBC-AP01 Vendor Master & Blacklist/Related Flags — vendor ID, tax ID, bank, related-party flag. XLSX.
- PBC-AP02 AP Subledger & Aging — supplier balances, terms; GR/IR clearing. XLSX.
- PBC-AP03 Top Suppliers Contracts — pricing, penalties, delivery incoterms. PDF.
- PBC-AP04 Three-Way Match Evidence (Sample Pulls) — PO, GRN, invoice. PDF.
- PBC-AP05 Expense Policies — travel/entertainment, approvals. PDF.

INVENTORY & COGS (IAS 2)
- PBC-INV01 Inventory Listing — SKU, location, quantity, cost method, NRV adjustments. XLSX.
- PBC-INV02 Cycle Counts / YE Count Instructions & Results — count sheets, variances, approvals. PDF/XLSX.
- PBC-INV03 Obsolescence Analysis — aging, slow-move criteria, write-down calc. XLSX.
- PBC-INV04 Standard Cost Build-ups / BOM — for manufacturing. XLSX/PDF.

PROPERTY, PLANT & EQUIPMENT / INTANGIBLES
- PBC-FA01 Fixed Asset Register — asset ID, location, useful life, depn method, additions/disposals, CWIP. XLSX.
- PBC-FA02 Supporting Docs for Additions/Disposals — PO, contracts, acceptance acts, sale docs. PDF.
- PBC-FA03 CapEx Approval Packs — board approvals, budgets. PDF.
- PBC-FA04 Impairment Assessment (IAS 36) — CGU model, assumptions, sensitivities. XLSX/PDF.
- PBC-FA05 Leases (IFRS 16) — lease register, contracts, payment schedules, incremental borrowing rate memo. XLSX/PDF.

EQUITY, BORROWINGS & FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS
- PBC-EQ01 Share Register & Changes — minutes, filings. PDF.
- PBC-DEBT01 Loan Agreements & Covenants — schedules, covenant calc packs, waivers. PDF/XLSX.
- PBC-DEBT02 Interest Schedules — accruals, EIR calcs if applicable. XLSX.

PROVISIONS, CONTINGENCIES & OTHER
- PBC-PROV01 Provisions Roll-forward — opening, add, used, released; basis memos (warranties, ARO, restructuring). XLSX/PDF.
- PBC-PROV02 Environmental/Decommissioning (IAS 37/IAS 16) — assumptions, discount rates. XLSX/PDF.

TAXES (IAS 12)
- PBC-TAX01 Corporate Income Tax Return & Trial Calc — current tax, adjustments. XLSX/PDF.
- PBC-TAX02 Deferred Tax Roll-forward — temporary differences, tax rates, lapses. XLSX.
- PBC-TAX03 VAT/GST/Sales Tax Reconciliations — to GL. XLSX.
- PBC-TAX04 Transfer Pricing Files — local/master file, intercompany agreements. PDF.

RELATED PARTIES (IAS 24)
- PBC-RP01 Related Party Register — entities, individuals, relationships, terms. XLSX.
- PBC-RP02 Intercompany Balances & Transactions — confirmations, eliminations. XLSX/PDF.

PAYROLL & HR (IAS 19)
- PBC-HR01 Payroll Master & Monthly Runs — gross/net, taxes, benefits. XLSX.
- PBC-HR02 Headcount & Org by Function — FTEs, joiners/leavers. XLSX.
- PBC-HR03 Bonuses/Share-based Plans — schemes, approvals, calcs. PDF/XLSX.
- PBC-HR04 Actuarial Reports (if post-employment) — assumptions, sensitivities. PDF.

IT GENERAL CONTROLS / DATA ACCESS
- PBC-IT01 ERP Access Listing (SoD flags) — users, roles, last login. XLSX.
- PBC-IT02 Change Management & Patch Logs — major releases affecting posting. PDF/XLSX.
- PBC-IT03 Data Dictionaries & Export Protocols — tables, field descriptions, extraction SQL if applicable. DOCX.

GOING CONCERN (ISA 570)
- PBC-GC01 Cash Flow Forecast 12+ months — base case + downside, covenant headroom. XLSX.
- PBC-GC02 Financing Plans — signed/refinancing status, term sheets. PDF.
- PBC-GC03 Sensitivity Models — shocks to revenue, margins, FX, rates. XLSX.

CONFIRMATIONS (EXTERNAL)
- PBC-CONF01 Bank, Legal, AR, AP, Loan, Lawyer — contact sheets and consent letters. XLSX/PDF.

MANAGEMENT REPRESENTATIONS (ISA 580)
- PBC-REP01 Draft Management Representation Letter — tailored to engagement scope and legal matters. DOCX.

INDUSTRY ADDENDA (PRESETS)

- PBC-INS03 Claims Triangle & Development — by LoB, AY/OY, IBNR calc rationale. XLSX.
- PBC-INS04 Reinsurance Agreements & Cessions — treaties, bordereaux, recoverables aging. PDF/XLSX.
- PBC-INS05 Liability Adequacy & Onerous Groups Assessment — memos, sensitivities. PDF/XLSX.

- PBC-ME02 Decommissioning/ARO Models — discount rates, timing, regulator filings. XLSX/PDF.

- PBC-ME04 Power/Fuel Purchase Agreements — take-or-pay, indexation clauses. PDF.

C. CONSTRUCTION / EPC
- PBC-CN01 Contract Ledger (IFRS 15) — contract assets/liabilities, WIP, performance obligations, variable consideration. XLSX.

D. DISTRIBUTION / FMCG
- PBC-FMCG01 Trade Terms Matrix — rebates, slotting fees, promos by key customers. XLSX.

PBC CALENDAR (PHASE-BASED)
--------------------------
Planning / Interim (PLN)
- GOV01-03, FS01-06, IT01-03, REV01-02, AP01-03, INV01/03, FA01/03, TAX01 draft, RP01
Year-End (YE)
- Full ledgers (FS04/05), reconciliations (CB03, AP02, REV03), confirmations setup (CONF01), inventory counts (INV02), lease packs (FA05), provisions (PROV01/02)
Close (CL)
- FS draft (FS06), impairment files (FA04), tax final (TAX01-03), going concern (GC01-03), legal letters (LEG02), subsequent events log (LEG04)
Bring-down (BR)
- Updates: LEG04, LEG02, CONF01 statuses, GC forecasts refresh, covenant calculations, post-YE bank statements (CB02)

-------------------------

RUSSIAN SUMMARY (для внутреннего пользования)
---------------------------------------------
PBC-лист — это мастер-перечень документов и данных, которые клиент обязан подготовить для аудита. В этом файле:
- Перечень по циклам (управление, юрвопросы, финансы, выручка/дебиторы, закупки/кредиторы, запасы, ОС/НМА, налоги, РП, ИТ, непрерывность).
- Жёсткие критерии качества: форматы, поля, сопоставимость, выверки.
- Календарь поставок по фазам: планирование, год-конец, закрытие, bring-down.
- Отраслевые приложения: страхование (IFRS 17), горная/энергетика, строительство/EPC, дистрибуция/FMCG.
- Связи с Legal Matrix, материалностью, KAM и шаблонами отчётности.
